----------------------------------------
Sloppy Joe
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    2 tbsp olive oil or coconut oil
    1 med onion, chopped
    1 med green bell pepper, chopped
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 lb ground beef
    1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
    1 tbsp chili powder
    1/2 tsp ground cumin

Directions

    - Heat large skillet over med-high heat

    - Add oil when hot

    - Add onion, green pepper, garlic. Saute until tender

    - Add ground beef. Continue to cook until beef browned, stirring

    - Stir in tomato sauce, chili powder & cumin. Cook five minutes.
