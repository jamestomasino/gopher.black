----------------------------------------
Paleo Tortillas
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    2 eggs
    1 tsp melted butter or coconut oil
    1/4 cup arrowroot powder
    1 tsp coconut flour
    pinch of salt
    1 tbsp water to make more spreadable

Directions

    - Crack eggs and mix with butter and water

    - Add dry ingredients and mix well

    - In a small (8") skillet, heat over medium heat

    - Pour in 1/3 of the mixture and immediately roll it around to evenly coat
      the pan

    - Cook 1 minute on each side

    - If storing, cool completely and store airtight in fridge
