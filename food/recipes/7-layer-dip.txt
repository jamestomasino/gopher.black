----------------------------------------
7-Layer Dip
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    9 oz Bean Dip
    11.5 oz Guacomole
    8 oz Sour Cream
    14.5oz Diced Tomatoes
    3.8 oz Sliced Black Olives
    8 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese (or Monterey Jack)
    3 Chopped Green Onions

Directions

    - Layer ingredients in order.

    - Chill. (use toothpicks to keep plastic wrap from getting messy)

    - Serve with scooping chips.
