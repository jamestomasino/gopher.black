----------------------------------------
Broccoli Casserole
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    2 (10 ounce) packages frozen broccoli, cooked and drained
    1 can cream of mushroom soup
    2 eggs
    8 oz. sharp cheese, grated
    3/4 cup of mayonnaise
    1 sleeve Ritz crackers

Directions

    - Crush the Ritz crackers into crumbs

    - Mix all the ingredients except the cracker crumbs

    - Butter a square or rectangular baking dish

    - Pour mixture into dish

    - Sprinkle the cracker crumbs on top

    - Dot the top with additional butter

    - Bake at 350 degrees for 35 covered with tin-foil

    - Uncover and bake an additional 15 minutes
