----------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Beef & Broccoli
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    2 lbs boneless beef (optional slice in strips)
    2 cups beef broth
    1 cup soy sauce
    1/3 cup granular swerve
    2 tbsp sesame oil
    6 garlic cloves, minced
    1 tsp xanthum gum
    1 large head fresh broccoli

Directions

    - Add soy sauce, broth, brown sugar, sesame oil, and garlic to slow cooker.
      Mix well.

    - Add beef, coat with mixture.

    - Cook on low 4-6 hours.

    - Remove 4 tbsp of sauce mix, place in small dish with xantham gum. Mix
      well. Pour back into slow cooker.

    - Add broccoli. Cook additional 30 minutes.
