----------------------------------------
Apple Crisp
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    5-6 apples
    1/8 tsp salt
    1 cup oatmeal
    1/3 cup butter
    1 tsp cinnamon sugar
    1/2 cup flour
    3/4 cup brown sugar

Directions

    - Slice apples (peeled or unpeeled)

    - Sprinkle cinnamon sugar over apples

    - Mix remaining ingredients & cover apples

    - Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes
