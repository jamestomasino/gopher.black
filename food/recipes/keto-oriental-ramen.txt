----------------------------------------
Keto Oriental Ramen
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    Spices

    1/4 tsp onion powder
    1/4 tsp ground ginger
    1/4 tsp garlic powder
    dash of black pepper
    1/2 chicken bouillon cube

    Contents

    (all optional, amounts to taste)
    zoodles, thin
    mushrooms, chopped
    red cabbage, thin sliced
    spinach, chopped

Directions

    - Add all ingredients to bowl.

    - Add boiling water.
