----------------------------------------
Keto Egg Hotdog Buns
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    3/4 cup almond flour
    1 1/2 tps baking powder
    3 eggs
    4 tbsp melted coconut oil

Directions

    - Mix eggs and oil

    - Mix flour + baking powder

    - Mix all into shaped dishes

    - Microwave 2-4 min

    - Let sit for 5 min to cool

    - I slice mine thin to serve. Place hotdog & toppings on top.
