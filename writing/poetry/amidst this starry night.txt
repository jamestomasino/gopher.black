walking briskly in wintered air
weeping branches dry and bare
sparkle tersely in moonlight's glow
amidst this starry night
shining, velveteen tableau
amidst this starry night

silhouettes of dewy eve
silent in this wooden greave
whisps of night bound for the fey
amidst this starry light
lilting, bounding in ballet
amidst this starry light
