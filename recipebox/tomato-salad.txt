----------------------------------------
Tomato Salad
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    4 ripe tomatoes
    1/4 c. red wine vinegar
    1/4 c. olive oil
    1 tsp. salt
    1 tsp. black pepper
    1 tsp. garlic powder
    1 tsp. basil
    1/8 tsp. oregano

Directions

    - Dice tomatoes into a large bowl.

    - Add all other ingredients.

    - Mix well.

    - Serve chilled.
