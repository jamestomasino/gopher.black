----------------------------------------
Slow Cooker Basic Chili
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    1 lb ground beef
    2 14 oz cans kidney beans, drained
    1 14 oz can diced tomatoes
    1 cup frozen onion/green peppers
    1 6oz can tomato paste
    1/4 cup brown sugar
    2 garlic cloves, chopped
    1 tsp oregano
    1 tsp cumin
    2 tsp chili powder
    1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions

    - Heap in the slow cooker, cook all day on low
