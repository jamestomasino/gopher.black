----------------------------------------
Keto Stuffed Peppers
----------------------------------------

Ingredients

    1 pkg turkey breakfast sausage links
    1 8oz Philly cream cheese
    1 can diced tomatoes (don't drain)
    Colored bell peppers (to fill)

Directions

    - Crumble and brown sausage
    - Add tomatoes (undrained)
    - Add cream cheese (cut into smaller pieces first)
    - Stir until melted/mixed
    - Add additional spices (I use oregano, pepper) as preferred
    - Microwave peppers for 5-6 minutes until soft
    - Pour sausage mixture into peppers & serve
